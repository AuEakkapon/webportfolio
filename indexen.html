About

Social science research was started by a group of social science faculty members in 1973 and in 1976 the university approved the establishment of an internal center. Faculty of Social Sciences called "Social Science Research Center

History

and received funding from the Faculty of Social Sciences Foundation/Agency both inside and outside the country When the scope of work has been expanded and the amount of work has increased therefore proposed to establish a Social Research Institute

Objective

To create academic excellence in Lanna cases To create multidisciplinary research results that benefit the community and respond to government policies. To provide academic services to students, academics, the private sector, communities and government agencies at the local level. and nationally and internationally It is the social information center of the North. to preserve the arts and culture and preserving natural resources and the environment

Philosophy, determination, vision, mission

Study the past to understand the present. Study the present to help build the future.

To be a leader in research and academic services in social-Lanna case at the national level
